Net Art

"The Closet"

"The Closet" tells a story about a girl and her history of past relationships which gets her thinking,

"What should I do?" I wanted to make sure that my audience understands that it's not always easy

to "come out of the closet" by explaining it through someone's personal experience. This shows that

Net Art could be used as a "diary" that encourages others to read about in order to understand

and empathize with the author. At the ending of the "game," I included websites that will help

inform the "player" of subjects used to create the overall story of the main character.

. . .

For my Net Art, I used JPGs and MP4s as visuals. I also embedded music in the background for

some choices. To create the visuals, I used Adobe Illustrator to create the backgrounds, and

Adobe Photoshop to add the JPGs of the silhouttes onto it. I also used Adobe Photoshop to create

the MP4s. This project was difficult to create since my laptop could not handle me making the MP4s.

In return, I could not really know how fast each video was going until I exported them. I could

have made GIFs, but I wanted to keep the "fade out" effect in the videos to give them more character.
