Video + Sound

"Remembering You"

"Tell a story, without telling a story."

. . .

My video was originally supposed to be someone who goes into depression and eventually drives

himself to commit suicide, but as I was editing my project even more, I decided to change my

theme to someone who lost their loved one due to a car accident. The video itself would seem like

it is about a boy who is remembering the good times he had with his girlfriend and has now broken

up with her. With the sound, however, it adds a more emotional aspect and changes the story to the

boy losing their loved one due to a car accident.

. . .

To create this video, I used videos and photos that were shot using my HTC ONE A9 cell phone.

I used Adobe Premiere to put everything together as well as crop them to make the camera angle changes

more fluid-like. Doing this project was somewhat difficult since my laptop could not handle the

application, but the ending result was perfectly fine to me.
