Remix Photo

"Ignorance"

For my Remix project, I wanted to make a Remix that showed one of the negative things about our society,

thus creating "Ignorance." My Remix art is based on the fact that we, as humans, have been destroying the

natural life that once used to surround us and act 'ignorant' to this for the living styles that we created for ourselves.

. . .

To create this artwork, I used different pictures that I pulled from Google Images and used the "masking" tool in PhotoShop

in order to make it look like one picture. I also used the "blending" tool to make the pictures appear seamless.

In PhotoShop, I also played with the image saturation settings to make the pictures "fit" each other even more.

This was my first time using PhotoShop, so I hope to improve my skills in the near future.
