Processing Art

"Self-Portrait"

At first I did not know how I wanted to go about on making my own self-portrait, but

I decided to make it simple, especially since I was practically teaching myself the

basics of Processing at home while I was in the middle of getting over a cold.

. . .

Using basic tools for Processing, such as "rect" and "ellipse," and giving them color,

I was able to create a small, cartoonish drawing of myself in Processing. It is not that

great, but it is great for me who learned processing for the first time.
